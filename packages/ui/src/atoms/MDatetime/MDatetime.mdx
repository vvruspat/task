## Customization

MDatetime composes `MDatepicker` and `MTimepicker`, each already themeable through
their own tokens. It exposes one token of its own:

| Token | Controls | Default |
|-------|----------|---------|
| `--datetime-gap` | Gap between the date and time pickers | `--gap-m` |

See **Guides/Theming & Customization** for the override mechanism.
