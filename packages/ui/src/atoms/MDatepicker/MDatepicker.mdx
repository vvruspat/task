## Customization

MDatepicker has no component tokens of its own — it composes `MInput`, `MButton`,
`MDropdown` and `MCalendar`, each already themeable through their own `--input-*` /
`--button-*` / `--dropdown-*` / `--calendar-*` tokens. See **Guides/Theming & Customization**.
